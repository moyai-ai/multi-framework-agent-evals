META QUEST 3 USER GUIDE

===========================================
INTRODUCTION TO META QUEST
===========================================

What is Meta Quest?

Meta Quest is a line of virtual reality (VR) headsets developed by Meta (formerly Facebook). The Meta Quest 3 is the latest generation standalone VR headset that combines virtual reality and mixed reality experiences without requiring a PC or external sensors.

Key Features:
- Standalone VR headset (no PC required)
- Mixed reality capabilities
- Advanced hand tracking technology
- High-resolution displays
- Powerful Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2 processor
- 6DOF (six degrees of freedom) tracking
- Extensive app and game library
- Wireless design for freedom of movement

===========================================
GETTING STARTED
===========================================

Initial Setup:

1. Charge Your Headset
   - Connect the charging cable to the headset
   - Plug into a power outlet
   - Allow at least 2 hours for full charge
   - LED indicator shows charging status

2. Download the Meta Quest Mobile App
   - Available on iOS and Android
   - Search for "Meta Quest" in App Store or Google Play
   - Install the app on your smartphone

3. Power On Your Headset
   - Press and hold the power button on the right side
   - Wait for the Meta logo to appear
   - Put on the headset

4. Follow the In-Headset Setup
   - Select your language
   - Connect to WiFi network
   - Log in to your Meta account (or create a new one)
   - Set up your guardian boundary
   - Adjust the headset fit and IPD (interpupillary distance)

===========================================
CONTROLLERS
===========================================

Meta Quest 3 Controllers:

The Meta Quest 3 comes with two Touch Plus controllers designed for intuitive VR interaction.

Controller Features:
- Ergonomic design for comfortable extended use
- Haptic feedback for immersive experiences
- Precision tracking using infrared sensors
- Multiple buttons and triggers for game controls
- AA battery powered (one battery per controller)

Controller Layout:

Right Controller:
- Trigger (index finger)
- Grip button (middle fingers)
- A and B buttons (thumb)
- Thumbstick (thumb)
- Meta button (home button)

Left Controller:
- Trigger (index finger)
- Grip button (middle fingers)
- X and Y buttons (thumb)
- Thumbstick (thumb)
- Menu button

Pairing Controllers:
1. Remove the battery cover
2. Insert AA battery
3. Replace battery cover
4. Turn on the headset
5. Controllers should pair automatically
6. If not, go to Settings > Devices > Pair New Controller

===========================================
HAND TRACKING
===========================================

Meta Quest 3 Hand Tracking:

The Meta Quest 3 features advanced hand tracking technology, allowing you to interact with VR experiences using just your hands - no controllers needed.

How Hand Tracking Works:
- Built-in cameras track your hand movements
- AI algorithms recognize hand poses and gestures
- Real-time rendering of virtual hands
- Supports natural gestures like pinching, pointing, and grabbing

Enabling Hand Tracking:
1. Go to Settings
2. Select Hands and Controllers
3. Toggle Hand Tracking to ON
4. Choose when to use hands vs. controllers

Supported Gestures:
- Pinch: Thumb and index finger together (select/grab)
- Point: Index finger extended (point at UI elements)
- Palm up: Open hand facing up (menu access)
- Thumbs up: Confirmation gesture
- Hand waving: System detection

Apps with Hand Tracking:
Many apps support hand tracking including:
- Meta Horizon Home
- Meta Browser
- Select games and experiences
- System menus and settings

===========================================
MIXED REALITY FEATURES
===========================================

Meta Quest 3 Mixed Reality:

Mixed reality (MR) blends virtual objects with your real-world environment using passthrough cameras.

Passthrough Technology:
- Full-color passthrough cameras
- High-resolution view of your surroundings
- Low latency for natural interaction
- Depth sensing for object placement

Mixed Reality Experiences:
- Games that use your real environment
- Productivity apps with virtual screens
- Fitness apps that use your space
- Creative apps for virtual art

Activating Passthrough:
- Double-tap the side of headset
- Press the Meta button and select Passthrough
- Set as default in Settings > Experimental Features

===========================================
CONNECTING TO WIFI
===========================================

WiFi Connection:

A stable WiFi connection is required for downloading apps, multiplayer gaming, and accessing online content.

Connecting to WiFi:
1. Open Settings from the main menu
2. Select WiFi
3. Choose your network from the list
4. Enter the WiFi password
5. Select Connect
6. Wait for connection confirmation

WiFi Requirements:
- 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz WiFi network
- WPA2 or WPA3 security
- Minimum 10 Mbps download speed recommended
- 50+ Mbps for high-quality streaming

Troubleshooting WiFi:
- Move closer to router
- Restart the headset
- Forget network and reconnect
- Check router settings
- Update headset software

===========================================
CHARGING AND BATTERY
===========================================

Charging Your Meta Quest 3:

Battery life varies based on usage, typically 1.5-2.5 hours for active gaming.

Charging Process:
1. Use the included USB-C charging cable
2. Connect to the headset charging port (left side)
3. Plug into USB-C power adapter (18W or higher recommended)
4. LED indicator shows charging status:
   - Orange: Charging
   - Green: Fully charged
   - Red: Low battery

Battery Tips:
- Charge before first use
- Store at 40-80% charge for long-term storage
- Avoid extreme temperatures during charging
- Can use while charging with extended cable

Battery Life Optimization:
- Lower screen brightness
- Reduce volume
- Close unused apps
- Disable hand tracking when not needed
- Use airplane mode for offline content

===========================================
GUARDIAN SYSTEM
===========================================

Guardian Boundary:

The Guardian system creates a virtual boundary to keep you safe while in VR.

Setting Up Guardian:
1. During setup or from Settings > Guardian
2. Choose Stationary or Roomscale
3. Trace your play area using the controller
4. Confirm the boundary
5. System saves the boundary for future use

Guardian Features:
- Visual grid appears when approaching boundary
- Color-coded warning levels
- Passthrough view when crossing boundary
- Multiple saved boundaries for different rooms
- Height adjustable floor level

Safety Tips:
- Clear the play area of obstacles
- Ensure adequate space (6.5 x 6.5 feet minimum for roomscale)
- Set up away from stairs, windows, and breakable objects
- Use a ceiling fan-safe space
- Check your surroundings regularly

===========================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
===========================================

Common Issues and Solutions:

Controllers Not Working:
- Check battery level
- Re-pair controllers in Settings
- Clean the tracking rings
- Restart the headset

Display Issues:
- Clean the lenses with microfiber cloth
- Adjust IPD slider
- Tighten head strap
- Check for software updates

Tracking Problems:
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Clean the external cameras
- Reset Guardian boundary
- Restart the headset

Audio Issues:
- Check volume settings
- Clean speaker areas
- Test with headphones
- Restart the headset

Performance Issues:
- Close background apps
- Clear cache in Settings
- Restart the headset
- Check available storage

Factory Reset:
1. Go to Settings > System
2. Select Factory Reset
3. Confirm the action
4. Headset will restart and erase all data
5. Set up as new device

CARE AND MAINTENANCE
===========================================

Headset Care:

Lens Cleaning:
- Use only dry microfiber cloth
- Wipe gently in circular motion
- Never use liquid cleaners
- Avoid touching lenses with fingers
- Store in protective case when not in use

Face Interface Cleaning:
- Remove silicone interface
- Wipe with damp cloth
- Mild soap if needed
- Air dry completely before reattaching
- Replace periodically for hygiene

Storage:
- Keep in cool, dry place
- Avoid direct sunlight on lenses
- Store in protective case
- Keep away from extreme temperatures
- Maintain 40-80% charge for long-term storage

Controller Maintenance:
- Replace batteries when low
- Clean tracking rings gently
- Store in controller holders
- Avoid dropping or impacts
- Update firmware regularly

===========================================
SAFETY AND HEALTH
===========================================

VR Safety Guidelines:

Physical Safety:
- Use in clear, safe play area
- Remain aware of surroundings
- Take breaks every 30 minutes
- Stop if experiencing discomfort
- Keep out of reach of children under 13

Health Considerations:
- Stop if experiencing nausea, dizziness, or discomfort
- Consult doctor if you have pre-existing conditions
- Start with shorter sessions
- Adjust IPD for comfortable viewing
- Ensure proper ventilation

Medical Conditions:
Consult physician before use if you have:
- Heart conditions
- Seizure disorders
- Vision problems
- Balance or equilibrium issues
- Psychiatric conditions
